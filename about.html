About Colin — the bench behind JewelFAQ

JewelFAQ is run by a single working bench jeweller and diamond setter based at 197A Warstone Lane in Birmingham’s Jewellery Quarter. For more than three decades I’ve made a living at the bench — repairing, remodelling and setting stones for both the trade and the public — and everything I tell you through JewelFAQ comes from that daily work, not from theory.

Bench jeweller & diamond setter

I started as a traditional goldsmith, then specialised in diamond setting in workshops across Europe. Over the years I’ve worked with pavé, micro pavé, channel, gypsy/flush and inlay setting on real customer jobs, not just samples.

Teacher & practical advisor

Alongside daily repair and setting work I run one‑to‑one courses at Diamond Setting School and advise other jewellers and small workshops. JewelFAQ is the place where all that experience is turned into clear, direct answers for your specific situation.

The work behind the advice

JewelFAQ doesn’t come from a meeting room. It comes from a real workshop bench, microscope and laser. These are the kinds of scenes that sit behind every answer I give you.

When you send in your case, it lands on this bench, not in a call centre. That is the whole point of JewelFAQ.

What JewelFAQ does NOT cover

To keep the advice sharp and fair to you, there are some areas JewelFAQ does not cover. Being clear about that upfront saves both of us time.

  • No manufacturing commissions or physical repairs.
  • No official expert reports for courts or insurance purposes.
  • No commercial referrals to workshops or suppliers.
