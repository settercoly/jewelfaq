Warning Signs of Fraud
• Messages from an unknown number or account claiming to be Colin or JewelFAQ.
• Requests to pay a “new” or “updated” link not sent from our official WhatsApp.
• Urgency pressure: “Pay now or lose your slot.”
• Requests for personal documents (passport, bank statements) — we never ask for these.

What to Do if You Suspect Fraud
1. Do not send any payment.
2. Contact us directly via WhatsApp at +44 74 1507 2425 to verify.
3. Report to Action Fraud (UK): actionfraud.police.uk — 0300 123 2040.
