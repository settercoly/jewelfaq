Frequently Asked Questions

Who answers my consultation?

Always me — Colin, bench jeweller and teacher with 30 years of experience. No assistants, no interns, no AI.

Do I have to pay before knowing if you can help?

No. You send your case for free. I review it, confirm I can help, and only then send you a payment link. You will never be charged for a case I can't address.

Why is the turnaround 24 hours?

Because I review each case carefully. My commitment is to reply within 24 hours of payment confirmation.

Will you reply instantly on WhatsApp?

No. WhatsApp is used to send you the payment link and follow up on your case. The consultation answer itself is delivered in writing within 24 hours.

Do you do calls or video calls?

No. I work exclusively by text and files. This lets me analyse every detail carefully and give you a precise written answer you can refer back to.

Is it confidential?

Completely. Your information, files and photos are used only to advise you. They are not published or retained long-term.
