Who JewelFAQ is for

If you work with jewellery and have a specific problem, JewelFAQ is built for you. These are the three most common groups I help.

Independent jewellers & setters

You're running a small workshop or trade bench and need a second pair of eyes. Maybe you're unsure about how to price a complex job, how to explain a risk to a customer, or whether a repair is even worth attempting. You can send photos from the bench, a short description and I'll help you decide on a safe, realistic way forward — including what to say to the client.

Designers & 3D modellers

You design in CAD or by hand and want to be sure your pieces will survive real-world wear and real-world repairs. You can send screenshots, STL previews or photos of prototypes, and I'll flag weak points, stone setting issues and practical changes before you commit to production.

Students & apprentices

You're learning stone setting or jewellery and need targeted, honest feedback. Maybe you've hit a wall with a specific technique or you're not sure which tools to invest in next. You send photos or videos of your work, I tell you what is going wrong, what is going right and what to focus on for your next 10 hours at the bench.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need professional photos?

No. Phone photos from the bench are usually better, because they show the real situation. If something is unclear I'll tell you what extra images I need.

Do I have to pay before knowing if you can help?

No. You send your case for free. I review it, confirm I can help, and only then send you a payment link. You will never be charged for a case I can't address.

Do you only work with people in the UK?

No. Most advice can be given remotely as long as you can send clear photos, videos and measurements. Time zones are easy to work around.

Which languages do you work in?

I work in English and Spanish. If you are more comfortable writing in another language, you can still send your case and we'll see what is possible.

Is this a course or a one-off consultation?

JewelFAQ is not a generic course. It is one-to-one advice applied to your specific case. If you want a full one- or two-week diamond setting course, that happens through Diamond Setting School instead.

Can I visit you in person?

For most people, online is faster and cheaper. If you are nearby or already coming to Birmingham, we can discuss an in-person consultation at 197A Warstone Lane.

Will you tell me the truth even if it's not what I want to hear?

Yes. My value is in honest technical judgement, not in telling you what you want to hear. If something seems off to me, I'll say so.

Is it confidential?

Completely. Your information, files and photos are used only to advise you. They are not published or retained long-term.
