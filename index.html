Frequently Asked Questions

Who answers my consultation?

Always me — Colin, bench jeweller and teacher. No assistants, no interns, no AI.

Why is the turnaround 24 hours?

Because I review each case carefully with the attention it deserves. Whatever the channel (WhatsApp, email or form), my commitment is to reply within 24 hours of payment confirmation.

Will you reply instantly if I message on WhatsApp?

No. WhatsApp is just a channel for sending your photos and files — the same as email or the form. Response time is up to 24 hours from payment confirmation.

What does the £50 fee cover?

One defined case. If the answer raises new questions or you add topics not in the original brief, those are treated as a new consultation.

Do you do calls or video calls?

No. I work exclusively by text and files. This lets me analyse the technical details with the care the craft requires and give you a precise answer you can refer back to whenever you need.

Is it confidential?

Completely. Your information, files and photos are used only to advise you. They are not published or stored long-term.
