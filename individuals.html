FAQ for individuals

Can you identify gemstones from photos?

No. Identifying gemstones from a photo is neither professional nor reliable. What I can do is analyse the setting, the technical risks, and the real complexity of the work — so you understand the case better and know what questions to ask.

Will you tell me the truth even if it's not what I want to hear?

Yes. My value is in honest technical judgement — not in telling you what you want to hear. If something seems off to me, I'll say so.

Can you tell me if a quote is expensive or cheap?

I can tell you whether the proposed work makes technical sense, whether anything important is missing, or whether there are inconsistencies. I don't judge other workshops' rates, but I will comment on the technical logic of the work.

Can you recommend a specific workshop or jeweller?

No. I don't make commercial referrals or earn commissions for directing work elsewhere. Here I offer only independent technical judgement.

Can I use your answer when speaking to the jeweller?

Yes — you can use the information to ask better questions and make more informed decisions. But this is not an official report or legal document.
