FAQ for jewellers & 3D designers

Do you review STL files?

Yes. I assess wall thickness, casting viability, critical zones and potential assembly problems — before you print or cast.

Can I ask about workshop problems, not just designs?

Yes. You can ask me about processes, workflow, client management and task organisation.

Are you responsible for the production outcome?

No. My advice is expert judgement based on years at the bench, but final execution depends on your suppliers and your own workshop.

Can you recommend casters, printers or suppliers?

No. I don't make commercial referrals or work on commission. I focus on giving you technical information and judgement.

Can we work together regularly on multiple projects?

Yes. That's exactly what the consultation packs are for — buy a pack of 10 or 100 consultations and use them as your projects require. See the Pricing page.
