Pricing FAQ

When do I pay?

You only pay once I confirm I can help and you agree to the format and price. There is no charge for sending your initial case description.

Why are prices shown as “from £X” rather than fixed?

Because the complexity varies. A quick question about a single repair is very different from a full review of a complex CAD design. Once I see your case I give you a clear, fixed price before you commit to anything.

Can I get a refund if I am not satisfied?

If I determine after payment that I cannot assist with your case, a full refund is issued. Once the written response has been delivered, no refund is issued unless there is a material factual error on my part.
