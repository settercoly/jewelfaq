Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who we are: JewelFAQ, trading as The Jewellery Crafters, Birmingham, UK.

What we collect: Your name, WhatsApp number, and the description of your jewellery case when you submit a consultation request.

Why we collect it: To provide the consultation service you requested, and to contact you about your case via WhatsApp.

Price: £50 per standard consultation, paid via Stripe.

How it works: You submit your case for free. Colin reviews it and confirms whether he can help. A payment link is sent only after confirmation. You are never charged for a case Colin cannot address.

Turnaround: Written answer delivered within 24 hours of payment confirmation.

Scope: Each consultation covers one defined case or question. New topics arising from the response are treated as a new consultation.

Refunds: If Colin determines he cannot help with your case after payment has been made, a full refund will be issued.

Format: Written answers only. No phone calls or video calls are included in any plan.
