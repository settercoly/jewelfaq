FAQ for students & aspiring jewellers

Is this a full course?

No. It's not a school or a structured programme. It's support and concrete answers as you move forward.

Can I send photos of my exercises for feedback?

Yes. Send me photos of your first pieces or assemblies and I'll tell you where the real technical issues are.

I want to set up my own workshop — can I ask about that?

Yes. You can ask about how to get started, what equipment to prioritise, how to organise your bench and what the real first steps are to start working or opening your own business.

Can you proofread or translate for me?

No. I work in English or Spanish only. If you need another language, you'll need to use your own translator.

Can I mix technical and business questions?

Yes, as long as they relate to jewellery, the bench or the workshop. I don't do general life coaching or topics outside the craft.
