Case received

Colin will review your case and contact you via WhatsApp.

You will only be asked to pay if Colin confirms he can help with your specific case. No surprises.

What happens next

  1. Colin reads your case — usually within a few hours.
  2. If he can help, he will message you on WhatsApp with a secure payment link (£50).
  3. Once paid, you receive a written professional opinion within 24 hours.
  4. If Colin cannot help with your specific case, he will let you know — no charge.
